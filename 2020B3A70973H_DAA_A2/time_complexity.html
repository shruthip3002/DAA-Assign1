    • Split Point Iteration: Within the nested loops, there exists an additional loop iterating over potential split points. This loop executes 'n' times, contributing O(n) time complexity. This involves simple computations that do not vary with the length of the RNA sequence. As a result, these operations contribute O(1) time complexity to each iteration of the split point loop.
    • Overall Time Complexity: Combining these factors, the overall time complexity of the algorithm is O(n^3). This is derived from the cubic relationship between the length of the RNA sequence and the time taken to complete the computation, considering all nested iterations and constant-time operations.
